Common Questions

FAQs

How long does a typical project take? ▼
It depends on the scope. A standard marketing website takes 2–4 weeks. A full e-commerce platform or custom web app is typically 6–12 weeks. We always provide a clear timeline in our proposal before any work begins.
Do you work with clients outside India? ▼
Absolutely. We have an active track record with UK clients and are fully set up for remote collaboration worldwide. We're comfortable with Slack, Zoom, Google Meet, and can accommodate UK business hours for calls.
How is pricing structured? Is it fixed or hourly? ▼
We primarily work on fixed-price scoped projects. After a discovery call, we'll send a written proposal with a clearly defined scope and fixed total cost — so there are no billing surprises. For ongoing maintenance, we offer monthly retainer plans.
What happens after the project is delivered? ▼
We offer 30 days of post-launch support for bug fixes included in every project. After that, you can stay on one of our monthly maintenance retainers for ongoing care, or engage us on a project-by-project basis as your needs evolve.
Do you provide hosting and domain management? ▼
Yes. We can set up and manage your hosting on AWS EC2, cPanel/WHM, or any other provider. We can also handle domain registration, DNS configuration, and SSL certificates as part of our deployment service.
Can you take over an existing project or codebase? ▼
Yes. We're experienced in code audits and taking over legacy projects. We'll review your existing codebase, identify what works and what needs improvement, and provide an honest assessment before any commitment from your side.
