Our Journey

Key Milestones

🚀
2021 — The Beginning
Devmora Technology Founded

Started as a freelance operation in Greater Noida, building custom React frontends for local businesses and startups. First client shipped in under 2 weeks.

🛒
2022 — First Major Build
Sellaids — Multi-Vendor E-Commerce

Delivered a full multi-vendor e-commerce platform with 1000+ products, Cloudinary CDN, admin dashboards, and vendor management panels built from scratch.

🏛️
2023 — Government Sector
NDR Portal — Directorate General of Hydrocarbons

Won and delivered a government-grade data portal on AWS EC2 with Sequelize ORM, CCAvenue payment integration, and interactive data catalogues. A landmark project for the agency.

🇬🇧
2023 — International Expansion
First UK Client — BRIT Commercials

Began our international journey with a UK truck dealership, delivering full SEO overhauls, GTmetrix Grade A performance, and a complete web retainer relationship that continues today.

🗺️
2024 — Present
Scaling Up & New Horizons

20+ projects delivered. Expanding service portfolio to include React Native mobile apps, advanced data visualisation, and enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure. Currently accepting new projects.
